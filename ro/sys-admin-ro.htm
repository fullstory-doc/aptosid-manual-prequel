Activarea serviciilor in sidux

sidux implicit ruleaza urmatoarele services : cpid, cron, dbus, hotkey-setup, kdm, klogd, sysklogd si li se va face update ori de cate ori se face dist-upgrade .Toate serviciile care sunt instalate dar inactive vor deveni active dupa dist-upgrade.

Pentru a porni o sesiune a unui serviciu : 

/etc/init.d/service start

sau

rcconf

pentru a activa un serviciu la startup :

Specificatii rcconf : Folositi : sagetile pentru a muta sus/jos , space pentru select/deselect , tab pentru inserat , ok sau cancel , `enter` aplica schimbarile

O alternativa la rcconf este:

update-rc.d service defaults

Parolele de root pierdute

Nu puteti recupera o parola pierduta dar puteti seta una noua .

Porniti de pe Live-CD.

Ca utilizator root montati partitia root (de exemplu /dev/hdb2)

# mount /dev/hdb2
Now chroot into your old root-Partition with
# chroot /media/hdb2
and use the passwd command to set a new password:
# passwd

Corectarea setarilor dpi - Filozofia de baza

Setarile DPI sunt greu de ghicit , dar la ora actuala sunt facute foarte corect de catre X, daca DisplaySize specifica dimensiunile fizice ale ecranului in "Monitor Section" din /etc/X11/xorg.conf .

Rezolutiile si ratele de refresh corecte

Orice monitor are propriile combinatii optime ale setarilor , dar, din pacate , nu toate monitoarele raporteaza corect valorile DCC , din aceasta cauza ,uneori , este nevoie de interventia manuala .

Driverele grafice corecte ale adaptoarelor

Unele placi video mai noi de la ATI si Nvidia pur si simplu nu lucreaza bine cu driverele free Xorg , in aceste cazuri singura solutie sunt driverele comerciale -closed source . Din motive legale sidux nu va instala implicit aceste drivere . Solutia - aici.

Selectia fontului implicit , a renderizarii si marimilor

sidux uses (Debian) pre-selected free fonts which have proved to be very balanced, your own fonts selection can/may deteriorate the quality of rendering. But there are a few powerful options in Debian (apart from settings in kcontrol) that can help providing spotless rendering with other fonts as well. But please be aware that every font has just a few real perfect sizes, other sizes may not play well.

After having changed font type or DPI (in X or Firefox/Iceweasel), you might need some readjustments to get results to your liking, especially. after a change from Bitmap Fonts to True Type Fonts or the other way round through:

dpkg-reconfigure fontconfig-config

If that does not work you might need to redo your fonts cache by:

apt-get install --reinstall --yes -o DPkg::Options::=--force-confmiss -o DPkg::Options::=--force-confnew fontconfig fontconfig-config
Pre-setari Firefox/Iceweasel

Aplicatiile GTK-based in general fac probleme in kde cu setarile implicite . Aceasta se poate rezolva prin :

apt-get install gtk2-engines gtk-qt-engine

In `control center` veti avea o noua linie de meniu gtk , setati aceasta sa foloseasca clearlooks iar in setarile de fonturi setati kde fonts.

In case of stability problems which occured in the past, but not since 2006 please remove or purge gtk-qt-engine, the settings are are kept in $HOME , and there are no dependencies which could be removed by it:

apt-get remove gtk-qt-engine
or
apt-get remove --purge gtk-qt-engine

Aceasta AR PUTEA rezolva problema legata de font rendering in aplicatiile gtk .
